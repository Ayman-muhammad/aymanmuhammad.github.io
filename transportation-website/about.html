Our Story

Founded in 2010, TransportCo has grown from a small local transport provider to one of Kenya's most trusted transportation and logistics companies.

We started with just three vehicles and a vision to transform how people and goods move across the region. Today, with a fleet of over 500 modern vehicles and a team of 200+ dedicated professionals, we continue to set new standards in safety, reliability, and customer service.

Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: every journey matters. Whether it's a daily commute, corporate transport, or critical cargo delivery, we treat each assignment with the importance it deserves.
