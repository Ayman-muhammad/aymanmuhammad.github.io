Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to booking requests?

We typically respond to booking requests within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, please call our 24/7 support line.

Do you provide transportation outside Nairobi?

Yes, we provide transportation services across Kenya and can arrange inter-city and cross-country transport.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, mobile money, and credit/debit cards for your convenience.

Can I track my cargo in real-time?

Yes, all our cargo shipments come with real-time GPS tracking accessible through our tracking portal.
