Professional Driver

Nairobi Full-time Drivers

We're seeking experienced professional drivers with excellent customer service skills and a commitment to safety. Join our team of transportation experts serving corporate clients and VIP customers.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Safely operate company vehicles following all traffic laws
  • Provide exceptional customer service to all passengers
  • Maintain vehicle cleanliness and perform basic inspections
  • Follow assigned routes and schedules precisely
  • Handle customer luggage and provide assistance as needed

Requirements:

  • Valid BCE driving license with PSV endorsement
  • Minimum 3 years professional driving experience
  • Clean driving record with no major violations
  • Excellent knowledge of Kenyan roads and traffic laws
  • Strong customer service and communication skills
  • Certificate of Good Conduct

We Offer:

  • Competitive salary with performance bonuses
  • Comprehensive medical insurance
  • Regular training and skill development
  • Modern, well-maintained vehicles
  • Career advancement opportunities

Logistics Coordinator

Nairobi Full-time Logistics

Coordinate and optimize our logistics operations to ensure efficient transportation of goods and timely delivery services. Work with our team to streamline supply chain processes.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Plan and coordinate delivery routes and schedules
  • Monitor shipment progress and provide customer updates
  • Coordinate with drivers, warehouses, and customers
  • Optimize logistics processes for cost efficiency
  • Maintain accurate records and generate reports

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Logistics, Supply Chain, or related field
  • 2+ years experience in logistics or supply chain management
  • Proficiency with logistics software and GPS systems
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and organizational abilities

We Offer:

  • Competitive salary package
  • Performance-based bonuses
  • Comprehensive benefits package
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Modern office environment

Fleet Maintenance Technician

Nairobi Full-time Maintenance

Keep our fleet in top condition! Perform maintenance, repairs, and inspections on our diverse vehicle fleet to ensure safety, reliability, and optimal performance.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform routine maintenance and repairs on fleet vehicles
  • Conduct safety inspections and diagnostics
  • Maintain maintenance records and documentation
  • Coordinate with parts suppliers and service providers
  • Ensure compliance with safety standards and regulations

  • Knowledge of various vehicle systems and diagnostics
  • Valid driving license
  • Attention to detail and commitment to quality
